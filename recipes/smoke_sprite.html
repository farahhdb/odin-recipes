Smoke Sprite

drink inspired by amazing So!YoOn! and RMs song yayyy

  • 4 oz smoky bourbon or whiskey
  • 7 oz sprite
  • ice and a cube of dry ice
  • lime or lemon wedge
  1. Fill a highball glass most of the way with ice.
  2. Pour in the bourbon first, then fill it the rest of the way with sprite.
  3. Serve with a lime or lemon wedge and a straw and enjoy!
